This page provides a list of the FHIR artifacts defined as part of this implementation guide.

Structures: Logical Models

These define data models that represent the domain covered by this implementation guide in more business-friendly terms than the underlying FHIR resources.

Acceptance Review

A review where acknowledgment decisions are made about plan elements
Acknowledgeable

Abstract pattern for elements that can be acknowledged or agreed upon
Activity Execution

An execution of a planned activity
Activity Outcome Observation

An observation characterizing the outcome of an activity execution
Barrier

A barrier that impedes progress toward goals or planned activities
Care Delivery Resource

A resource used in care delivery
Care Plan Manifestation

A manifestation of a care plan - a specific instance of a care plan at a point in time
Care Plan Patient

The patient who is the subject of the care plan
Care Team

A team of individuals involved in the patient's care. For more details, consult the Care Team Domain Analysis Model.
Care Team Member

A member of the care team participating in the care plan
Communication

A communication event related to the care plan
Communication Thread

A thread of related communications
Composable

Abstract pattern for elements that can be organized hierarchically
Concern

A health concern or condition addressed by the care plan
Expressible

Abstract pattern for elements that can be expressed by a care team member
Goal

A goal in the care plan. Possible relationship to concern, planned activity, performed activity, observation.
Guideline

A clinical guideline that informs the care plan
Health Risk

A health risk identified for the patient
Modification

A modification made to a plan element
Organization

An organization involved in care delivery
Outcome Review

A review assessing outcomes of performable elements
Performable

Abstract pattern for elements that have a performance lifecycle
Plan Definition

A definition or template for care plans
Plan Element

Abstract base for all care plan content elements. All plan elements are specific to a Patient.
Planned Activity

An activity planned as part of the care plan
Preference

A patient or care team preference that informs care planning
Priority

Abstract pattern for elements that can be prioritized
Protective Factor

A factor that protects against health risks or barriers
Reconciliation Act

An act of reconciling plan elements
Reconciliation Log

A log of reconciliation activities. Reconciliation merges versions of a plan maintained in different places or merges diverse specific plans into overarching plans.
Resource Requirement

A requirement for resources to perform an activity
Review

Abstract pattern for review activities
Revisable

Abstract pattern for elements that can be revised over time
